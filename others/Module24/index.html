Booking Terms

All bookings are subject to availability. A deposit of 30% is required at the time of booking, with the balance due 45 days prior to departure.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 45 days prior to departure will receive a full refund minus a $100 administration fee. Cancellations made between 45-30 days prior will forfeit the deposit. Cancellations within 30 days are non-refundable.

Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to cover unforeseen circumstances including medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost luggage.

Health & Safety

Travelers are responsible for ensuring they meet all health requirements for their destination, including vaccinations and COVID-19 protocols.
